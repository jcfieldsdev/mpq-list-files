Progress

Accurate for Starcraft: Brood War retail version 1.13e.

All official Blizzard maps (the ones that came with Starcraft and Brood War, plus their weekly and monthly releases and the Precursor campaign) are included.

In instances where an archive exists for both Starcraft and Brood War with the same name, the syntax is “original|expansion.”

Created with version 1.00 (Windows) of the Starcraft disc and versions 1.04 (Windows) and 1.05 (Windows and Mac OS) of the Brood War disc.

  • Brood War is available in many languages, all of which have their information in the same MPQ, all under the same file names as their English counterparts. Abbreviations used:

    • DEU—German
    • ENU—English (U.S.)
    • ESP—Spanish
    • FRA—French
    • ITA—Italian
    • PTB—Portuguese (Brazilian)
  • There are two files named “(listfile)”: the first is detected by MPQ Editor and contains a partial list of the archive’s contents. The second is detected by MPQ Viewer (“unk0041.xxx” in MPQ Editor) and is a list of files added by the installer.
